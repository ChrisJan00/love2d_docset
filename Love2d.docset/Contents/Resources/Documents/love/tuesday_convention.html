Tuesday Convention

The Tuesday Convention establishes the following names for the following people:

  • Developer - Developer of LÖVE, makes the engine
  • Lover - Uses LÖVE (to make games)
  • User - Uses whatever the lovers make

The Scrolls

The ancient scrolls cover the Tuesday Convention, the translation follows:

At the Tuesday Convention it was said,
what names certain people had.
Developers are those who make love,
and lovers are those who make stuff,
users are then the people who play.
This terminology, was set on that day.
