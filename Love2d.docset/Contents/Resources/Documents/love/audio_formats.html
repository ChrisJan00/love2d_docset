Audio Formats

As of Love 0.9.1, the following audio formats are supported:

  • WAV
  • MP3
  • Ogg Vorbis
  • MOD formats including "699", "abc", "amf", "ams", "dbm", "dmf", "dsm", "far", "it", "j2b", "mdl", "med", "mid", "mod", "mt2", "mtm", "okt", "pat", "psm", "s3m", "stm", "ult", "umx", "xm",
