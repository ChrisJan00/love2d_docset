Lope2D

Lope2D is a wrap over Love2D physics engine API. It makes dealing with the physics API easier and faster.

Most of beginners who wants to make his own game based on physics, usually have problems using its API. Bodies, shapes, fixtures. A chaos of methods, isn't it? Here the Lope comes.
